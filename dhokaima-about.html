About


This complex dates back to the 1920s, when it was used as horse carriage house, stable, cowshed and offices for a Rana palace. Much of the complex retains its original architectural features of wood, mud-mortar. brick, tile, and lime pointing and embellishments. The outerwall of the Dhokaima outdoors is part of the original city walls of Patan town, and overhead is a tall and spreading walnut tree.
